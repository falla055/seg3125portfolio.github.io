About me

Hi, I'm Felix! I am a 4th year Computer Science student, currently also working as a Data Scientist for the Government of Canada. At the moment, I am completing what should be my last semester of classes if all goes well.

Some of my favorite subjects include Data Structures, Database Systems, Algorithm Analysis, and Programming Paradigms. While I could go into detail about what made each course particularly enjoyable, what stands out is how significantly they have contributed to my career development.

Though I do enjoy the challenge of programming and solving data related problems, I also really enjoy the sun and spending time outside. Whenever I can free myself fron my desk, you can find me at the gym, taking a walk or spending time with my family.

A fun fact about me is that I secretly love girly music... Taylor Swift, Katy Perry, Shania Twain and more recently Sabrina Carpenter and Tate McRae!
